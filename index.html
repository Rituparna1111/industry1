Create buisness plan

A business plan is an essential roadmap for business success. A business plan provides direction, keeps you on track and is usually a requirement when you seek finance. A business plan is a formal grid map of all of your business goals, and plans on how to achieve them. Writing your plan is not the end of your planning process because it is a never ending process.The plan should take consideration your particular business and its environment. Create a business that will help you achieve your primary aim and Plan a business that will be a good corporate citizen.

Government schemes for startups

CGTSME Scheme

CGTSME is one of the biggest Startup Loan Schemes launched by the Ministry of MSME in India.Under this Government scheme, a collateral-free loan is provided to eligible startups and MSMEs.The loan is dispersed via a trust named Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises.

CLCSS Scheme

the creation of the Credit Linked Capital Subsidy for Technology Upgradation (CLCSS) Government scheme, wherein Govt provides financial help to MSMEs to upgrade their technology and implement state of an art technological platforms for their business.

ZED Scheme

Focussed on existing and new manufacturing units, ZED or Zero Defect and Zero Effect mission is to encourage manufacturers to create better products, with high quality and zero defects.The government scheme will provide both financial support, and technology and tools to ensure zero defects in their products.

Micro Scale Industries

Micro Scale Industries are those industries in which the manufacturing, production and rendering of services are done on a micro scale. These industries make a one-time investment in machinery, plant, and equipment.The owner of these industries is actively involved in the day-to-day activities of the business.

Small scale Industries

Essentially the small scale industries are generally comprised of those industries which manufacture, produce and render services with the help of small machines and less manpower. These industries are generally labour-intensive, and hence they play an important role in the creation of employment.

Medium Scale Industries

Medium Scale Industry means an industrial undertaking where more than twenty and less than one hundred workers are manufacturing or processing goods with the aid of power or without the aid of power.These Industries and businesses contribute considerably to the employment,and manufacturing output of the state.

State Government wise Investment Policy

The government has been providing considerable support and funding to startups that are looking to develop,
expand or start new operations.It offers a number of startup funding opportunities with several small and
medium business grants, loans, and subsidies.

First level of investment

First stage of funding is often the earliest stage of funding, with a business owner's personal funds or initial angel investment rounds.During this stage, investors provide startups with capital to begin developing products in exchange for equity.

Second level of investment

The capital raised during a second stage is usually intended to capitalize the company for 6 months to 2 years as it develops its products, performs initial marketing and branding, hires its initial employees, and undertakes early stage business operations.

Third level of investment

In this level of investment buisness is already quite successful,These companies look for additional funding in order to help them develop new products, expand into new markets and focused on scaling and growing as quickly and as successfully as possible.

Job Opportunities

As business grows and expands, it will require the services of more people. This way, many people will get jobs,
thereby reducing the unemployment problem up to an extent. Moreover, it will be easier for people to secure jobs
in startups and small companies.

  • Small businesses tend to hire employees who live near the communities in which they operate, thereby boosting local employment rates. With their earnings, workers purchase goods and services, so money is fueled back into other local businesses.

  • If small businesses crop up every day, they will always require new employees to expand their team. So, freshers will get a golden opportunity to find employment opportunities matching their skills, interests, and qualifications.

  • Entrepreneurs provide a large number of employment opportunities to the people to manage their business activities. They generate direct as well as indirect employment to the unemployed persons by setting up more and more units of production at small- and large-scale levels.
